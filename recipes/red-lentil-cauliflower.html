Red Lentil Cauliflower

Description

Healthy and comforting red lentil curry

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp grapeseed or peanut oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 large chile pepper (jalapeno or serrano), minced
  • 2 large shallots
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece of fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 large parsnip peeled and chopped
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 ground coriander
  • 1 1/2 cups red lentils, sorted and rinsed
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth or water
  • 1 1/2 - 2 pounds cauliflower (about one medium sized head) , trimmed and sliced into small florets
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • tbsp lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Have all of the ingredients chopped and readily at hand
  2. In a large stockpot, heat the oil over medium heat
  3. Saute the onion and shallots until tender and translucent, 5 to 7 minutes
  4. Add the grated ginger and chile, and saute for 1 minute
  5. Add the spices and briskly stir-fry for 30 seconds, then add the parsnip and stir-fry for another minute
  6. Slowly pour in the vegetable broth, then stir in the lentils
  7. Cover the pot, raise the heat to high, boil for 1 minute
  8. Give the mixture a stir, then cover the pot and lower the heat to medium-low.
  9. Allow the lentils to simmer for 10 to 12 minutes
  10. They should turn light yellow and look mushy
  11. Add the cauliflower florets, stirring to coat with the lentils
  12. Partially cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes, until the cauliflower is tender but not completely falling apart
  13. Remove from the heat and stir in chopped cilantro, lime juice and salt
  14. Allo the curry to sit, covered, for about 15 minutes before serving to allow the flavours to meld and the mixture to cool slightly
