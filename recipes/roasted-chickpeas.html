Roasted Chickpeas

Description

Delicious oven roasted chickpeas. An easy to make simple snack that is perfect for travelling or just to enjoy with a glass of wine.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas or 1 cup of cooked chickpeas
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celsius
  2. In a bowl, add the chickpeas and olive oil and mix until the chickpeas are well coated in oil.
  3. Add the salt, pepper and garlic powder and stir until the chickpeas are all coated with the spices.
  4. Place the chickpeas on a baking tray and put in the oven for 15 mins, shake the try after half the time for an even roast.
  5. Enjoy!
