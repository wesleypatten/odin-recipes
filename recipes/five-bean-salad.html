Five Bean Salad

Description

Five bean salad is a refreshing, protein-packed dish that’s perfect for meal prep and gatherings. Combining kidney beans, black beans, chickpeas, green beans, and cannellini beans with chopped bell peppers, red onion, and parsley creates a colorful, nutritious mix. A tangy dressing of olive oil, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, and honey enhances the flavors. Letting it marinate in the fridge for an hour deepens the taste, making it even better the next day. This salad is great on its own, as a side dish, or in wraps. It’s versatile, packed with fiber, and can be easily customized.

Ingredients
  • 1 can (400g) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (400g) green beans, drained
  • 1 can (400g) cannellini beans (or butter beans), drained and rinsed
  • ½ small red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup (75g) red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ cup (75g) yellow bell pepper, diced
  • ¼ cup (15g) fresh parsley, chopped
Dressing
  • ¼ cup (60ml) olive oil
  • 3 tbsp apple cider vinegar (or red wine vinegar)
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp honey (or maple syrup for vegan)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
Instructions
  1. Prepare the beans: Drain and rinse all the beans (except green beans) under cold water. Drain green beans and pat dry.
  2. Mix the salad: In a large bowl, combine all the beans, red onion, bell peppers, and parsley.
  3. Make the dressing: Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl or jar.
  4. Combine: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything well to coat.
  5. Chill: Let it marinate in the fridge for at least 1 hour (or overnight) for the best flavor.
