Pinto Bean Wraps

Description

Great as a lunch or dinner, tasty pinto beans and rice wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1 can or cup of cooked pinto beans
  • 1 cup of basmait rice, rinsed
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 pound mushrooms, chopped
  • 1/2 sweet red pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 pound celery, finely chopped
  • 1 plum tomato, chopped
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tsbp tex mex spice
  • 1/2 pound cabbage, chopped
  • 2 large carrots, grated
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsbp olive oil
  • Tortilla wraps

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan add half the olive oil with heat at medium.
  2. Add the cumin and the tumeric as the oli starts to bubble, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the rice and stir until its coated in the spices.
  4. Add two cups of water and raise heat to high until the water starts to boil. Low heat and leave to simmer for 8 minutes.
  5. Put the rest of the oil in a medium saucepan, add the onions and fry until the onions are lightly brown.
  6. Add the mushrooms and sweet red pepper, fry for amother 5 minutes. Add the tomato and stir-fry in the tex mex spice. Season with salt for taste
  7. Add the cabbage, carrots and vegan mayonnaise together in a bowl add salt for taste
  8. Heat the Tortilla wraps in the microwave under the grill
  9. Add rice, beans and sald to the wrap
  10. Enjoy
